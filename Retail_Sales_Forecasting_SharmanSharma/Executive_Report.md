# Executive Business Report

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze historical retail sales data and build forecasting models that can help businesses estimate future demand and improve inventory planning.

---

## Dataset

The project uses the Retail Superstore sales dataset provided by XYLOFY AI.

Total Records: 9800

---

## Key Findings

- Technology category generated strong sales performance.
- West region demonstrated consistently high sales.
- Monthly sales showed seasonal variations.
- SARIMA, Prophet, and XGBoost were compared for forecasting accuracy.
- Anomaly detection identified unusual sales spikes.
- K-Means clustering grouped product categories into demand segments.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-demand categories.
- Maintain additional stock in strong-performing regions.
- Monitor anomaly periods to identify business opportunities.
- Use the best-performing forecasting model for demand planning.

---

## Conclusion

Machine learning and statistical forecasting techniques significantly improve demand prediction and support better inventory planning, operational efficiency, and business decision-making.
